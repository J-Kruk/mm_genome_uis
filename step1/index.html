Culture Genome: Visual Representations of Cultural Knowledge

Instructions

The goal of this task is to collect cultural knowledge that can be represented by images, directly from members of the community.

You will be presented with a cultural concept, a question, and an example answer. Your task is to (1) critique how this concept, theme, or idea is represented by the example answer, and (2) contribute a better image and justification for the cultural concept.


Task Descriptions

  • For the first question, let us know if the concept, theme, or idea exists in your culture. Select yes or no.
    • If no, let us know what would be the closest practice or belief to the concept shown.
  • The second question will ask if you believe the image is a good representation of the concept in your culture. Select yes or no.
    • If no, let us know what about this image fails to represent this concept in 1 - 3 sentences.
  • In the third question we ask that you use the Google Image Search tool on this page to select another image for this concept.
    • Select an image that you believe effectively represents this concept or idea in your culture.
    • Only select images through this tool, do not upload any personal photographs from your computer.
  • The final question will ask you to provide a description of the image you selected.
    • Why do you believe this image is a good representation of this concept in your culture?
    • Give your answer in 2 - 3 sentences.
